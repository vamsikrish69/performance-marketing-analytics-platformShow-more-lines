# Updated Low Performing Campaigns

## Business Question

Which campaigns are the weakest relative performers based on ROAS, ROI, and spend?

## SQL File

analyses/updt_low_prfmnc_campaign.sql

## Business Value

This query ranks campaigns from lowest to highest ROAS and ROI. Even when no campaign has negative ROI or ROAS below 1, this analysis helps marketing teams identify campaigns that may need optimization compared to stronger performers.
