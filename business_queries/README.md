# Business Queries

This section contains business-focused analytical use cases built on top of the Performance Marketing Analytics Platform.

The queries use data generated in the staging, intermediate, and marts layers to answer key marketing and customer acquisition questions.

## Included Business Queries

### 1. Top Campaigns by ROAS
Identifies the highest-performing campaigns based on Return on Advertising Spend (ROAS).

### 2. Channel Performance Summary
Compares marketing channels such as Google Ads and Meta Ads across spend, revenue, ROAS, ROI, and conversion metrics.

### 3. Customer Acquisition Analysis
Analyzes customer acquisition behavior, revenue generation, and customer value segmentation.

### 4. Low Performing Campaigns
Identifies campaigns generating poor performance and lower returns on advertising investments.

## Business Value

These analyses help marketing teams:

- Optimize advertising budgets
- Improve campaign effectiveness
- Identify underperforming campaigns
- Understand customer acquisition trends
- Improve return on marketing investments
- Support data-driven decision making
